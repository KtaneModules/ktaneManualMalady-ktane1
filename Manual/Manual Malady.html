On the Subject of Manual Malady

M'lady, my manual, a melody in malady.

The module presents an array of eight rainbow-coloured buttons, a green play button and a red submit button below.

Each coloured button represents a section of an audio clip about the description of a module. Press the play button to hear each part from left to right.

This module in question would also show its name in the screen briefly when the bomb is activated before glitching out.

Initially, the sections are all scrambled up. To solve the module, sort the sections such that the full audio clip can be heard properly from left to right, then press submit.

Press two coloured buttons above to swap them. Press the play button when the module is playing its audio will stop it.

A strike will be given for an incorrect arrangement but will not reset the module. The name of the chosen module would also display itself in the screen briefly before glitching out once again.
